Karla has used Ditto to order shoes for her upcoming fashion show. As she is a university graduate and has little to no money to fund her very first collection she used Ditto to order fake shoes at a cheaper rate. She also chose Ditto because she was able to customise the prints, colours and materials to suit her collection, as it is very eclectic. She ordered seven pairs for models, and they all have a variety of shoe sizes from a 6-10.
